SwilPOS

In a world driven by efficiency and user-centric solutions, SwilSORT seeks to revolutionize how businesses streamline their operations.

  • View Figma

Context

SwilSORT is a order management application. It empower businesses with intuitive order tracking, real-time communication, and seamless order processing.

Project Goal

  • Streamline and optimize the order management process to reduce manual tasks, minimize errors, and improve the overall operational efficiency for businesses.
  • Enable seamless communication between customers, suppliers, and the business, ensuring instant updates and order status tracking.
  • Create an intuitive and user-friendly interface that caters to a diverse user base, ensuring accessibility and inclusivity for users of varying technical backgrounds and abilities.

Problem statement

  • Struggle with a time-consuming and error-prone order processing system, leading to delayed order fulfillment, increased operational costs, and frustrated customers.
  • Ability to provide customers with real-time updates on their order status, causing uncertainty and dissatisfaction among their clientele.
  • Complex and unintuitive user interfaces, making it challenging for users to navigate, resulting in training overhead and decreased productivity.

solutions

  • We Implemented an automated order processing system that reduced manual intervention, minimizes errors, and speed up order fulfillment.
  • We Developed a feature for real-time order status updates, providing customers with transparency and reducing uncertainty.
  • We designed the user interface with a focus on simplicity and user-friendliness, reducing training time and improving productivity.

Style Guide

  • Pink is known to evoke emotions like comfort, warmth, and happiness. It can create a positive emotional connection with application
  • Pink against a white background offers a strong contrast, which can enhance readability and make important elements or call-to-action buttons stand out. This can help users quickly identify key functions within your app.
  • For typography, I used a classical serif font "Urbanist".
