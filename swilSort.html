SwilSORT

swilSORT is a inventory management application, designed to streamline processes, enhance user experience, and empower businesses to make smarter decisions in an ever-evolving marketplace.

  • View Figma

Context

swilSORT simplifies the complex task of inventory tracking and ensures a delightful user experience, ultimately optimizing operational efficiency for businesses of all sizes.

Project Goal

  • Our project goals were centered around enhancing the efficiency of inventory management by developing an intuitive and user-friendly application.
  • The primary objectives of this project were to create an inventory management application that simplifies the task of inventory tracking.
  • Empower users with a feature-rich and visually appealing application.

Problem statement

  • The problem at hand is the lack of a modern inventory management tool tailored to the needs of businesses today. This absence results in operational challenges, compromised data accuracy, and a lack of actionable insights.
  • This project seeks ability to maintain optimal stock levels and meet customer demands and create a more user-centric solution.

Research

  • To build a solid foundation for our project, we engaged in extensive market research, studying various inventory management solutions and identifying gaps in the current landscape.

Style Guide

  • Deep teal is often associated with professionalism and modernity. It can convey a sense of trust and reliability, which is important for an inventory management application
  • White background provides high contrast, which enhances text and content readability. Deep teal text on a white background ensures that information is legible and easy to understand, even in different lighting conditions.
  • For typography, I used a classical serif font "Inter".
