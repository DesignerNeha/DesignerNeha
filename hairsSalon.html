HairsSalon

HairsSalon mobile application aims to streamline the salon appointment booking process, enhancing the user experience for both customers and salon owners.

  • View Figma

Project Overview

HairsSalon is a online salon booking application with the goal of improving user satisfaction, reducing booking errors, and increasing salon occupancy rates

Project Goal

  • The primary goal is to significantly improve the overall user experience for both salon customers and owners.
  • Increase the efficiency of the booking system by reducing booking errors and double bookings.
  • Support salon owners in optimizing their operations and growing their businesses.

Problem statement

  • Customers often struggle to find available slots, understand service options, and encounter errors in the booking flow, resulting in a poor user experience.
  • Salon owners and staff face difficulties in managing appointments efficiently.

Style Guide

  • Style tiles is a collection of visual information (colours, fonts and icons) to maintain consistency in design.
  • I used Green and orange color combination. Green and orange can evoke positive emotions such as freshness, growth, energy, and enthusiasm.
  • For typography, I used a classical serif font "Manrope".
